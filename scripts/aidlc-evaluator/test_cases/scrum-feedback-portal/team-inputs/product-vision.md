# PulseBoard — Product Vision (Team-Owned)

## Problem

Employees lack a lightweight, low-friction way to send structured feedback to
leadership, and leadership lacks a single place to review it.

## Vision

A simple internal portal where employees submit categorized feedback and admins
review it, growing over time into a live, insight-rich feedback tool.

## Releases

- **Release 1 (MVP)**: submit + review + SSO.
- **Release 2**: live results dashboard, sentiment tagging, weekly digest email.

## Non-goals

- Public (external) feedback.
- Anonymous moderation workflows (may revisit later).

## Personas

- **Employee** — submits feedback.
- **Admin (People team)** — reviews feedback and, later, monitors trends.
